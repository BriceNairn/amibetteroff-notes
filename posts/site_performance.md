## Metrics Overview

### Launch

AmIBetterOff.au was initially shared through a Reddit post targeting Australian technology communities.

The launch produced the first meaninful wave of real user traffic and helped validate that the calculator solved a genuine problem for contractors comparing permanent employment against contract rates.


**Early Observations**
- Reddit generated the first major spike in traffic and search impressions
- Users immediately begain sharing the calculator organically in comment sections and discussions
- Search Console begain indexing the site shortly after launch
- Long-tail search queries started appearing earlier than expected
- Traffic persisted beyond the initial Reddit spike, suggesting ongoing discovery through Google Search

**Metrics**
Initial launch metrics showed
- Early Reddit driven traffic spike
- ~670 unique visitors through Cloudflare Analytics during the initial launch
- First indexed impressions and clicks appearing in Google Search Console shortly after posting

**Insight**
The launch validated that
- there was a demand for clearer contractor vs permanent salary comparisons in Australia
- simple utility tools can gain traction through community sharing before formal marketing
- SEO momentum can begin surprisingly early whena  tool directly answers search intent
> The initial Reddit launch effectively acted as both product validation and the starting point for organic search discovery.
