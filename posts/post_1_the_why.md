# 1 — The Why
> An Exercise of Internet Archaeology, Conservation & Resurrection

## About me
I personally have worked a cumlative 5.5 years on contract, and while this helped me build a home deposit in my 20s, it wasn't all smooth sailing. 
Every year while on contract the business enforced mandatory Christmas shutdowns which meant as a contractor I wasn't getting paid, and there was more mental pressure around giving myself a break and going on leave.

Early I was often confronted with the un-easy feeling of not knowing whether my contract would be renewed or not, but it is something I adjusted to after the 2nd year of rolling 6-month and 12-month contracts. 
I was blind-sided when my contract was not renewed in the 5th year. 
Which happened just after I was hit by and struggling with recovery, and had moved out for the first time and undertaken a mortgage.

  >Stressful times

## Inspiration
I came across an online calculator that I had found on some Australian finance sub-reddit called MoneyHead.app 

  >**Warning!** Please do not go to this site, 
  MoneyHead.app was not maintained by its creator
  It is now serving malware and nefarious spam).
  
  >You can view the orignal site through the internet archive [wayback machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20230501000000*/moneyhead.app).



This tool really opened up my eyes to the reality and nature of contracting work and how a larger hourly rate may not be all it’s cracked up to be when you consider what you're not being paid for.

Back when I joined the job search again in 2024 I went back to my faithful comparison tool to help me make an informed decision, only to find that the domain went to a domain auction site, it looked as though the previous owner had not maintained their payments.

I considered trying to buy the domain through the auction and re-building the site there, but after a chat with chat-gpt it became clear that I might get into legal trouble for doing so.

The website wasn’t totally gone though, I was able to go back in time and look at it again as a reference while building my site through the Internet archive, Wayback Machine.

This was the launching point for the inspiration for **[AmIBetterOff.au](https://amibertteroff.au)** 


## Circumstances
At the time just prior to starting this project

- I found myself unemployed after a contract ended, 
- processing the end of a long-term relationship, 
- and had already spent much too much time deep-diving the Epstein files.

Building this project was the escape that I needed.

  >I was left with a lot of energy, all the time in the world and no entertainment budget, this project became the outlet to get me through this rough time.


## Intention
I wanted to create a new calculator site, with equations using updated tax brackets, include simplified calculations of the medicare levy, and give a user plenty of options to customise how this tool is best used to give them more clarity over what an apples-to-apples comparison of salary & contract pay has to offer.

Also noting that the previous site had gone under, I wanted to build this tool transparently, and seek community funding to keep the tool alive.

I also wanted the final product to be something that is easy on the eyes, as I have accessibility problems with bright screens.

Also, it’s been a pretty hard slog here in Australia over the last couple of years, anything that helps the battlers get a better deal, and negotiate higher pay is the win everyone needs right now to keep going.

I hope that the community will see the effort and value such a tool brings, and that they’ll pay it forward to keep it going.
